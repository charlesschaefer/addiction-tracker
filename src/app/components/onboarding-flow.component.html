Welcome to Addiction Tracker

Your personal journey to wellness starts here. This app is designed to help you track, understand, and overcome substance use challenges.

Home Screen Overview

Your home screen is your command center. From here, you can access all features and see your sobriety progress at a glance.

Sobriety counter at the top

Recording Usage

Tap the "+" button to record substance use. Be honest with yourself - tracking accurately helps you understand your patterns.

Track substance, amount, and cost
Record mood and craving intensity
Identify your personal triggers

Motivational Factors

Add personal motivations for each substance to remind yourself why you want to quit. These will appear when you're about to record usage.

Add text, images, or audio

Getting Started

Here's how to begin your recovery journey with our app:

  1. First: Add your substances and motivational factors
  2. Then: Record usage when it happens
  3. When tempted: Try alternative activities
  4. Regularly: Check your dashboard to track progress
  5. Celebrate: Watch your achievements grow!
